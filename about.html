About Us

Innovating the Agricultural Industry with Sustainable Solutions

Who We Are

OSP Bulk Pty Ltd is a South African-based, 100% Black-Owned agricultural company. Founded in 2023 by Mr. Mbongeni Phiri, we are committed to harnessing the power of South Africa’s natural resources and advancing innovative farming techniques. Our company’s vision is to empower communities and contribute to food security while revolutionizing the agricultural industry.

Our Mission

To become a one-stop shop for agricultural crop production and innovative farming systems, delivering high-quality products and services that elevate the agricultural industry and benefit local communities.

Our Vision

"Aim for the Stars" – We envision becoming one of the top agricultural companies in the world, all while uplifting communities in rural areas and promoting sustainable farming practices.

Our Values

  • Ubuntu: Building relationships based on mutual respect and community growth.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and transparency in all our business dealings.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving and evolving our farming techniques.
  • Sustainability: Ensuring long-term success through eco-friendly agricultural practices.

Meet the Founder

Mbongeni Phiri

Mbongeni Phiri, the founder of OSP Bulk Pty Ltd, holds a certificate of competence as a Barista from Ciro Coffee Academy. With a passion for agriculture and community development, Mr. Phiri established OSP Bulk to create sustainable solutions and empower rural communities through farming and employment opportunities.
